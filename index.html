New Dreams Ltd. by Laserdisc Visions
Honestly, what can I say about this album that hasn't already been said before?
Yes, this IS a precursor to Vektroid's delving into the vaporwave scene. But, that doesn't necessarily mean that it's automatically considered good by default. Firstly, let's talk about the contents within the album.

THE CONTENTS

We start off with a rather hazy track: "Mind Access" It's actually rather soothing to listen to in the short span of a minute or two. It's a simple sampling repetition of "(JINGLE) Pia Over" by Naofumi Hataya, one of many composers I assume of the game NiGHTS Into Dreams. It's a simple, yet effective way of introducing this odd segahaze vaporwave album. "Malls" then begins to play. This time with a rather intriguing mix of japanese commercials overlayed by what sounds like something you'd hera in an actual mall. Or 'malls', I guess you would say. Lol. Then comes in "Dual". I like this track a lot, with the only gripe of it being that it's very shortlived. :( "Idgaf Island", a rather touching tongue-in-cheek name for a track, starts playing. If you haven't already guessed it by now, some of these tracks are rather short and repetitive with seemingly no real addage other than the Jap-comm mix in the background. I guess that would act as a bit of a distraction for the ears if Ramona thought that her tracks weren't good enough already, but, eh. What can you do, anyhow?
